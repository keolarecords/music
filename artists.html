Sun Berri

Sun Berri is an independent music producer, recording artist of electronic dance music, self-published author, and founder of Keola Records. She has been producing independent creative projects since 2022. Her musical influences include Timecop1983, Black Eyed Peas, and Depeche Mode. Ana is proficient with FL Studio digital audio workstation, enjoys basic coding with VS Studio, and an art enthusiast creating designs using Canva application.
