Keola Records

Keola Records is an independent electropop music label based in the US, founded by Ana Keola, also known as Sun Berri. Keola Records was established in 2023 for the purpose of creating a new wave of electropop dance music, elevating the genre across Southeast Asia, and providing select music creators an opportunity to sample tracks from Sun Berri's catalog with production and distribution support. Investors, creators, and innovators of all backgrounds who are interested to learn more about Keola Records are welcome to connect.
