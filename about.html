About

Background

Keola Records is an independent electropop music label based in the US. The label was established in 2023 by Ana Keola, also known as Sun Berri.

Purpose

Creating a new wave of electropop dance music and elevating the genre across Southeast Asia from providing select music creators the opportunity to sample tracks from Sun Berri's catalog and receive production, promotional, and distribution support. The purpose is to help aspiring music creators build a professional portfolio with a general understanding of collaboration, networking, and hands-on experience without financial or exclusive obligations to the label.

Values

Keola Records values transparency, kindness, creativity, ambition, and gratitude that begins with personal choices and ends with a memorable emotion that is felt from the last beat of a song. To pursue every day with faith in one's authenticity and everlasting courage to lead with empathy and honesty are personal choices that reflect respect. Music becomes a respected art when it is crafted by a soul who chooses to live respectfully.

Mission

The mission at Keola Records is to encourage, support, and promote creativity within a unique community of passionate, kind, and smart entrepreneurs.
