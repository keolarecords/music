Keola Records

Keola Records is an independent electronic dance music label based in the US and the official home of producer, recording artist, self-published author, and founder Ana Keola, also known as Sun Berri. Keola Records was eestablished in 2023, initially as a private label for Sun Berri's works. Two years later in September of 2025, Keola Records opened the label to give selected independent music creators an opportunity to sample tracks from Sun Berri's catalog with provided vocal mixing and distribution at no cost to the music creators. This decision was made by Sun Berri to help aspiring creators build their portfolio while gaining experience with networking, collaborating, and getting featured. Keola Records offers the Keola Records Creators Guide, an entertaining and helpful e-book to music creators that can be purchased and downloaded.
