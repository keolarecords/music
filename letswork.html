Let's Work

With Keola Records

Keola Record is seeking independent music creators who are looking to network, collaborate, and build their portfolios. Creators must be at least 21 years of age and registered with a Performing Rights Organization. Creators looking for music to enhance their creative projects are welcome to inquire.

For consideration to work with Keola Records, follow Sun Berri on Instagram @sunberrimusic and send a dm with a link to your creative works. Keola Records will allow selected music creators to sample any track from Sun Berri's catalog and consider providing vocal mixing, publishing, and distribution for modified samples that align with our creative values and artistic approach (see section below). Creators must provide a cover art and song title for their modified version of the sample. Vocals must be original and recorded with minimal background noise. Lyrics must original and non-explicit. Producers who wish to participate must use FL Studio.

Tracks from Sun Berri's catalog are non-exclusive. All samples belong to Keola Records and cannot be redistributed, reproduced, sold, licensed, or used without consent from Keola Records. Selected artists and producers will be featured on Keola Records website, credited as a composer and publisher (if applicable) with 50/50 share on digital streaming and royalties collected from the Performing Rights Organization. Keola Records reserves all rights.

There are no upfront costs for music creators to sample Sun Berri tracks and no associated costs for selected works that are released and featured. Creators wishing to use Sun Berri tracks in their projects may inquire on a general sync licence for a low-cost and upfront fee.

Send inquiries and share your creativity with Sun Berri on Instagram @sunberrimusic


Creative Values & Artistic Approach

Music creators wishing to sample Sun Berri tracks that align with Keola Records creative values and artistic approach may purchase the Keola Records Creators Guide E-book written by Sun Berri. Tap the red link or access the e-book from the home page.
