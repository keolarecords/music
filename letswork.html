Let's Work

With Keola Records

Keola Record is seeking independent music creators who are looking to network, collaborate, and build their portfolios. Creators must be at least 21 years of age and registered with a Performing Rights Organization. Creators looking for music to enhance their creative projects are welcome to inquire.

For consideration to work with Keola Records, follow Sun Berri on Instagram @sunberrimusic and send a dm with a link to your creative works. Keola Records will allow selected music creators to sample any track from Sun Berri's catalog and consider providing production and distribution support for samples that align with our creative values and artistic approach. Creators must provide a cover art and song title for their modified version of the sample. Vocals must be original and recorded with minimal background noise. Lyrics must be original and non-explicit. Producers who wish to participate must use FL Studio. All creators are invited to purchase and download the Keola Records Creators Guide e-book to understand the creative values and artistic approaches that create the culture at Keola Records.

Tracks from Sun Berri's catalog are non-exclusive and non-transferrable. All rights to submitted works belongs to Keola Records. Selected artists and producers will be featured on Keola Records website, credited as a composer and publisher (if applicable) with 50/50 share on digital streaming and royalties collected from the Performing Rights Organization.

There are no upfront costs for music creators to sample Sun Berri tracks and no associated costs for selected works that are released and featured. Creators wishing to use Sun Berri tracks in their projects may inquire on a general sync licence.

Creators Benefits

Featured creators on Keola Records gain the blessing to work with Sun Berri without obligations to the label and continue to receive promotional benefits, music credits and royalty shares with the grace to reference Keola Records on their resume while continuing to work with other creators to build their portfolio.
