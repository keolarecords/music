Let's Work

With Keola Records

Keola Record is looking for independent music creators who are looking to network, collaborate, and build their portfolios. Creators must be at least 21 years of age and registered with a Performing Rights Organization.

To participate, the artist must follow Sun Berri on Instagram @sunberrimusic and send a direct message with a link to a video or post showcasing the artist's ability to sing to an orginal song written by the artist. Keola Records will make an immediate decision upon reviewing the artist's creativity to allow the artist to sample any Sun Berri track with original vocals and lyrics.

Upon reviewing the artist's sample, Keola Records will consider providing vocal mixing and publishing to release the track. There are no upfront cost, guaranteed payments, or obligations. This is an opportunity for independent music creators to gain experience with collaboration and get featured on real tracks to showcase their work. All productions belong to Keola Records and all participating creators will receive credit for their works. Selected artist(s) to be featured on a track released by Keola Records will split equal shares with the producer and be featured on Keola Records website. Tracks are non-exclusive for participating music creators and cannot be distributed by the participant.

Send inquiries and share your creativity with Sun Berri on Instagram @sunberrimusic
